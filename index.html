Hi there, I'm Justin!

Hey there, I'm Justin! I'm currently a sophomore EECS major at UC Berkeley, but I've always been a Bay Area kid, born and raised in the South Bay. I decided to build this website because I wanted a way to share some of my thoughts and opinions in a personal blog.

When I am not digging my way out of CS problem sets and projects, I am a lead undergraduate researcher at the Berkeley Institute of Data Science, working on a project to crowd-source data for machine learning on invasive species. In addition, I am an undergraduate researcher and Linux systems administrator for Berkeley's Automation Lab. I am also a project developer for Blueprint, a campus organization that provides pro bono tech consulting to nonprofits nationwide.
